STREE 2

  • 👉🏻About

    Stree 2 is the highly anticipated sequel to the 2018 blockbuster Stree, a horror-comedy that became a surprise hit for its unique blend of supernatural elements and humor. Directed by Amar Kaushik and produced by Dinesh Vijan, the sequel is expected to bring back the original cast, including Rajkummar Rao, Shraddha Kapoor, and Pankaj Tripathi, who were instrumental in the first film's success.

  • 👉🏻Review

    Stree 2, the much-anticipated sequel to Stree (2018), delivers a mix of horror and comedy, though with varying results. Rajkummar Rao shines once again as Vicky, balancing fear and humor, while Abhishek Banerjee stands out with his comedic timing. Shraddha Kapoor, though present, has less screen time. The film builds on its predecessor's universe but struggles with pacing and predictability at times. However, it offers enough scares and laughs to entertain fans of the first movie​ Hindustan Times.

  • 👉🏻Cast

      The cast of Stree 2 (2024) includes a mix of returning and new faces. The main cast features:
    • Rajkummar Rao as "Vicky"
    • Shraddha Kapoor as "The Girl with No Name" (the mysterious woman from the first film)
    • Pankaj Tripathi as "Rudra"
    • Abhishek Banerjee as "Jana"
    • Aparshakti Khurana as "Bittu"

  • 👉🏻Earning

    Stree 2 has performed phenomenally at the box office, becoming one of the highest-grossing Hindi films of all time. As of late September 2024, the movie has grossed over ₹612 crore, with a net domestic collection of approximately ₹581.5 crore. This impressive figure includes ₹291.65 crore in its first week alone. Despite a slight drop in collections toward the end of its theatrical run, it continues to be a major box office success, raking in substantial profits against its modest budget of ₹60 crore​(Koimoi)​(FilmiBeat).Additionally, Stree 2 has also begun streaming on OTT platforms, further extending its revenuestreams​(FilmiBeat).
